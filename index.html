    • Background

  • The Radiology Reports task is a process that translates radiographs into text to provide an overview of the process of obtaining detailed findings & thoughtful impressions. It has recently been attracting attention by creating a computer-based diagnosis that reduces the work burden of radiologists.

  • Radiology Reports Generation Tasks have a new challenge: generating accurate reports that reliably describe lesions is difficult due to incorrect correlations within image-text data caused by visual and linguistic bias.

  • A new challenge is to consider cause and effect relationships to generate accurate reports that reliably describe lesions, and we constructed a dataset to meet this need.




    • Introduction

  • We utilize an LLM-based Transformer to annotate datasets containing cause-and-effect relationships.

  • In addition, we would like to construct a transformer that can classify the cause of diagnosis (Q5) according to the input data.
